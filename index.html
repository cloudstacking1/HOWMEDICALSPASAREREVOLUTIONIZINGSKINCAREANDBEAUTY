What is a Medical Spa?

A medical spa (often referred to as a med spa) is a hybrid between a traditional day spa and a medical clinic. These establishments combine relaxation and therapeutic services with advanced medical treatments. Theyre typically supervised by licensed healthcare professionals, offering services that go beyond what youd typically find at a day spa.
